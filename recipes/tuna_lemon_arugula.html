Grilled Tuna Over Argula, Lemon Vinaigrette

Description

Lemony, zesty, tuna goodness. This meal has minimal prep and cook time making for an easy to process very healthy lunch.

Ingredients

  • 5 oz sashimi tuna, sushi grade
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp capers
  • kosher salt and fresh pepper
  • 2 cups baby arugula

Steps

  1. Season tuna with kosher salt and fresh cracked pepper.
  2. Place arugula and capers on a plate. Combine oil and lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Heat your grill or grill pan to high heat and clean grate well.
  4. When grill is hot, spray grate with oil to prevent sticking then place tuna on the grill; cook one minute without moving.
  5. Turn over and cook an additional 1 minute; remove from heat and set aside on a plate.
  6. Slice tuna on the diagonal and place on top of salad. Top with lemon vinaigrette and eat immediately.
